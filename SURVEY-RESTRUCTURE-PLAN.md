## The Four Customer Segments

### EXISTING CUSTOMERS
**Definition:** Currently own and use an indoor growing system
**Focus:** Satisfaction, usage patterns, retention risk
**Size Estimate:** ~5-10% of target market

### TIER 1: "Soon-to-be" Noncustomers
**Definition:**
- Know about indoor growing systems
- Have seriously considered buying one
- Either bought and abandoned OR almost bought but didn't

**Characteristics (from research):**
- Minimal purchasers ready to jump ship
- Price-sensitive
- Waiting for better alternative
- On the edge of the market

**Examples:**
- Netflix users who left cable TV
- People considering smart gardens but haven't pulled trigger

**Focus:** What's stopping them? What would convert them?
**Size Estimate:** ~15-25% of target market

### TIER 2: "Refusing" Noncustomers
**Definition:**
- Know about indoor growing systems
- Have NOT seriously considered buying
- Consciously chose alternative solutions

**Characteristics (from research):**
- Found better alternatives
- Consider it unacceptable/unaffordable/too complicated
- Deliberate choice, not ignorance

**Examples:**
- People who grow herbs in pots on windowsill
- Farmers market shoppers who prefer outdoor-grown
- People satisfied with grocery store produce

**Focus:** Why did they reject it? What alternatives work better?
**Size Estimate:** ~30-40% of target market

### TIER 3: "Unexplored" Noncustomers
**Definition:**
- Never heard of indoor growing systems
- Farthest from the market
- Untapped latent demand

**Characteristics (from research):**
- Never considered this solution
- May have unmet needs
- Represent vast ocean of potential

**Examples:**
- People focused on health but unaware of solutions
- Apartment dwellers who assume they can't grow food
- Supplement users who don't know they could grow medicinal herbs

**Focus:** Current needs, alternative solutions, latent demand
**Size Estimate:** ~40-50% of target market

## Multi-Choice Logic: "None of these" Auto-Deselect

**JavaScript Logic:**

```javascript
function toggleOption(option, isNoneOption = false) {
    if (isNoneOption) {
        // If "None" is clicked, deselect all others
        if (option.selected) {
            selectedOptions.forEach(opt => {
                if (opt !== option) opt.selected = false;
            });
        }
    } else {
        // If any other option is clicked, deselect "None"
        const noneOption = findOption("None of these");
        if (noneOption) noneOption.selected = false;
    }
}
```

**Apply to all multi-select questions with "None" option:**
- Reasons not purchased
- What would make you buy
- Current food solutions
- Demographics
- Wellness activities
- Grow interests
